Girl Child Advocacy

Girl Child Advocacy Ensure Every Girl Child Thrives

Girl Child Advocacy(GCA) is a non-governmental organization committed to bridging the educational gap for orphaned girls in the northern Ghana. We envision a world every girl get a chance to reach her full potential.

Get Started

What Do We Offer

The GCA (Girl Child Advocacy) Inclusion Program is dedicated to empowering orphaned girls who have dropped out of school or never had the chance to attend. Our research in rural communities in northern Ghana confirmed that this issue persists, with many such cases in disadvantaged areas. We are currently supporting four of these girls through apprenticeship programs!

Rented Apartment for Girls

We provide comfortable housing in a rented apartment, ensuring a safe and secure environment.

Provision of Basic Needs

We provide them all their basic needs such as food , accommodation, electricity, water and many more.

Mentorship Programs

Our dedicated mentors guide them through personal and reproductive health education, along with navigating daily life challenges.

Our Vision

Our ultimate vision is to establish a permanent home and skills development center. This center will offer a supportive and nurturing environment where girls can explore and develop a wide range of skills tailored to their individual passions, empowering them to create brighter and more promising futures.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make a transformative impact on the lives of orphaned girls in northern Ghana by providing them with the resources, support, and opportunities they need to overcome challenges, realize their potential, and lead empowered, fulfilling lives.

Meet Our Team

Our team is driven by a share vision: a world where every girl can thrive. With expertise in education, social work, and community development, we collabrate to create lasting change and ensure girls have the support they need to succeed.
